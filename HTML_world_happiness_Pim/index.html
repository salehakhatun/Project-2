Project Outline:

Our group aimed to explore the happiness rankings of countries across the world, focusing on specific factors such as the freedom to make life choices, social support and GDP per capita. In addition, we analysed the life expectancy rankings of countries worldwide. Further analysis can be performed to determine any correlations between happiness factors and countries with higher life expectancies.

Data Source
