World Happiness Score vs Social Support & Generosity

Examining the relationship between Social Support and World Happiness Score, we can observe a strong positive correlation. This indicates that countries that provide greater social support have individuals with higher happiness scores.

In contrast, the measure of Generosity does not appear to correlate to the country's Happiness Score. Countries with high happiness scores correspond with lower generosity levels, whilst countries like Myanmar and Haiti with the highest generosity levels score less than 5 on the happiness scale.
